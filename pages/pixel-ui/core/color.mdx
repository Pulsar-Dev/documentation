# Colors ![](http://localhost:3000/client.png)

##### `PIXEL.DecToHex(dec, zeros)`
  Converts a decimal to a hexadecimal

---
##### `PIXEL.ColorToHex(color)`
  Converts a RGB color to hexadecimal

---
##### `PIXEL.ColorToHSL(color)`
  Converts a RGB color to HSL

---
##### `PIXEL.HSLToColor(color)`
  Converts a HSL color to RGB

---
##### `PIXEL.CopyColor(color)`
  Copies a color?

---
##### `PIXEL.OffsetColor(color, offset)`
  Offsets a color by a set ammount

---
##### `PIXEL.HexToColor(hex)`
  Converts a hexadecimal color to RGB

---
##### `PIXEL.GetRainbowColor()`
  Gets a rainbow color that updates

---
##### `PIXEL.IsColorLight(color)`
  Checks if a color is light

---
##### `PIXEL.LerpColor(time, from, to)`
  Changes one color to another color over a period of time

---
##### `PIXEL.IsColorEqualTo(from, to)`
  Checks if one color is the same as another
