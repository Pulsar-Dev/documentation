# Text ![](http://localhost:3000/client.png)

##### `PIXEL.DrawSimpleText(text, font, x, y, col, xAlign, yAlign)`
  Draws text on the screen

---
##### `PIXEL.DrawText(text, font, x, y, col, xAlign, yAlign)`
  PIXEL.DrawSimpleText but different

---
##### `PIXEL.DrawShadowText(text, font, x, y, col, xAlign, yAlign, depth, shadow)`
  Draws text with a shadow

---
##### `PIXEL.DrawDualText(title, subtitle, x, y, h)`
  Draws 2 texts

---
##### `PIXEL.WrapText(text, width, font)`
  Wraps text if it is too long

---

##### `PIXEL.EllipsesText(text, width, font)`
  Ellipses text if it is too long
