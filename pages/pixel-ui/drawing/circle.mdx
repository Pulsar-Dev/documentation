# Circles ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Pulsar-Dev/documentation/main/public/client.png)

##### `PIXEL.DrawCircle(x, y, w, h, col)`
  Draws a circle

---
##### `PIXEL.CreateCircle(x, y, ang, seg, pct, radius)`
  Creates a mad circle

---
##### `PIXEL.DrawCircleUncached(x, y, ang, seg, pct, radius)`
  Creates a mad circle but uncached
